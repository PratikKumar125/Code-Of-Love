My Dearest Love

I know words may not be enough, but I want you to know how deeply sorry I am.

Like Shah Rukh Khan said in 'Kal Ho Naa Ho':

"Pyaar karna har kisi ko aata hai, ache se pyaar karna sirf kuch logon ko aata hai."

You've shown me what true love means through your countless compromises:

Your patience, your understanding, and your unwavering support.

I promise to cherish you and make up for my mistakes.

You are my Simran, and I'll be your Raj. ❤️

Please forgive me, my love.
